Soundproofing guidance

Derived from the live reference comparison.

Capture a reference first so the analyzer can rank problem frequencies against a stable baseline.

Measurement recipe
  1. Place a speaker at the noise source and play a repeatable broadband signal.
  2. Capture a reference outside the room or before treatment.
  3. Move to the protected side or retest after each change.
  4. Watch which octave bands stay close to the reference. Those are the weak links.
